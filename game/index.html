Jacob Gramm Gren — Danish language teacher, writer and endurance runner

Jacob Gramm Gren is a Danish language teacher specialising in Dansk som andetsprog (Danish as a second language), with 7+ years of experience teaching adult learners. Until 2026 he was a language consultant (sprogkonsulent) at UCplus Dansk in Denmark; he now lives in San José, Costa Rica, where he is available for language-teaching roles — onsite or remote. He is the creator of Dansk Plus (danskplus.dk), a Danish-grammar learning app for iOS and Android.

Experience

  • Language teacher / consultant (sprogkonsulent), UCplus Dansk — Danish as a second language, adult learners.
  • Elected staff representative (tillidsrepraesentant) for colleagues.
  • Previously taught at Scenekunsthojskolen Snoghoj, a Danish residential college for the performing arts.
  • 7+ years designing and refreshing curricula and exam-aligned classroom materials.

Education

  • University of Southern Denmark (SDU) — Dansk som Andetsprog (Danish as a Second Language), 2024–2026.

Languages

Danish (native), English (fluent), Spanish (working proficiency, improving).

Beyond the classroom

Endurance runner (Silkeborg Atletik Klub, SAK 77), heavy metal vinyl collector, and an aspiring author of language-education books and learning materials.

Contact

Portfolio and writing: grammgren.dk. To discuss a teaching role, email grammgren@proton.me, or use the “Hire Jacob” link on this page.
